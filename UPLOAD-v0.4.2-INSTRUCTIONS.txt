Cassette Motion Pro v0.4.2
==========================

This update improves saving in the Bike Fit Workspace.

What changed:
- The workspace saves the current fit session when you close it.
- The bottom button now says Save & Close.
- The save message explains that sessions live in the client Measurements folder.
- The v0.4.1 Body Angles overlay fix is still included.

Upload steps:

1. Extract Cassette-Motion-Pro-0.4.2-save-update.zip.
2. Open the extracted folder.
3. Upload everything INSIDE the extracted folder to the GitHub repository root.
4. Commit with: Add v0.4.2 safer workspace saving

Then edit .github/workflows/build.yml and change VERSION to 0.4.2. Commit the
change and wait for the Actions run to receive a green check.

Test checklist:

1. Open a client.
2. Open the Bike Fit Workspace.
3. Enter notes or measurements.
4. Click Save & Close.
5. Reopen the same client and workspace.
6. Confirm the session and values are still there.
7. Test Measure Before Video and Measure After Video again.
