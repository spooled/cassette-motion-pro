Cassette Motion Pro v0.3.2 Update
==================================

This update replaces Left/Right video with Before/After video.

1. Extract Cassette-Motion-Pro-0.3.2-update.zip.
2. Open the extracted folder. Upload its contents, not the outer folder.
3. In GitHub choose Add file, then Upload files.
4. Drag everything from INSIDE the extracted folder onto GitHub.
5. Confirm paths begin with README.md, docs/, installer/, and src/.
6. Commit with: Add v0.3.2 before and after video comparison

Then open:
https://github.com/spooled/cassette-motion-pro/blob/main/.github/workflows/build.yml

Click the pencil and change VERSION: 0.3.1 to VERSION: 0.3.2, then commit.
Download the 0.3.2 installer after the final Actions run receives a green check.
