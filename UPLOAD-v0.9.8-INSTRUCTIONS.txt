Cassette Motion Pro v0.9.8 - Scrollable Guided Capture

This update fixes the Guided Capture window when the right side is too tall
for the screen.

What changed:

- The Guided Capture right-side controls now scroll vertically.
- Save to Before and Save to After are reachable on smaller screens.
- The Close button stays pinned at the bottom of the window.
- Keeps v0.9.7 Guided Capture report trace.
- Keeps the saddle setback convention:
  - Behind BB = negative
  - In front of BB = positive
- Updates app/build/installer/report versions to 0.9.8.

Upload steps:

1. Open your GitHub repository:
   https://github.com/spooled/cassette-motion-pro

2. Upload everything from this package to the repo root.

   Important:
   - Include the hidden .github folder.
   - The workflow file must end up here:

     .github/workflows/build.yml

3. Commit with this message:

   Add v0.9.8 scrollable guided capture

4. Go to Actions.

5. Open the newest run.

6. Download:

   CassetteMotionPro-Windows-0.9.8

What to test:

1. Open Bike Fit Workspace.

3. Choose a Measurement image.
4. Open Guided Capture.
5. If the right panel is too tall, use the scrollbar on the right panel.
6. Confirm you can reach Save to Before and Save to After.
