Cassette Motion Pro v0.7.2
==========================

This update adds the first real click-to-measure Bike Metrics workflow.

What changed:
- Keeps the v0.7.1 one-button Before + After image combine.
- Replaces the Image Measurement Assistant placeholder with a real workflow.
- Click two calibration points and enter the known length in millimeters.
- Click two measurement points and review the calculated result.
- Save the result directly to the selected Bike Metrics row as Before or After.

Upload steps:

1. Extract Cassette-Motion-Pro-0.7.2-click-measure-bike-metrics.zip.
2. Open the extracted folder.
3. Upload everything INSIDE the extracted folder to the GitHub repository root.
4. Commit with: Add v0.7.2 click measure bike metrics

Then edit .github/workflows/build.yml and change VERSION to 0.7.2. Commit the
change and wait for the Actions run to receive a green check.

Test checklist:

1. Open a client.
2. Open the Bike Fit Workspace.
3. Open Report Images.
4. Choose a Before image.
5. Choose an After image.
6. Click Combine Before + After.
7. Confirm the Side-by-side image field fills in.
8. Open Bike Metrics.
9. Confirm the Measurement image field also uses the combined side-by-side image.
10. Click Assist on Saddle height or another bike metric.
11. Click Calibrate Scale.
12. Click two points with a known real-world length.
13. Enter the known length in millimeters.
14. Click Measure Points.
15. Click the two measurement points.
16. Click Save to Before or Save to After.
17. Confirm the Bike Metrics value fills in and the session saves.
