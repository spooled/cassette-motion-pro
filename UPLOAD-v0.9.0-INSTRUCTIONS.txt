Cassette Motion Pro v0.9.0 - Guided Landmark Capture

This update adds the first guided bike measurement workflow.

What changed:

- Adds a Guided Capture button at the top of the Bike Metrics tab.
- Uses the selected Measurement image.
- You calibrate the scale once.
- Then you click four landmarks:

  1. Bottom bracket center
  2. Saddle top
  3. Saddle tip
  4. Grip / hood contact point

- The app calculates and fills:

  Saddle height
  Saddle setback
  Saddle tip to grip reach
  Handlebar X
  Handlebar Y

- You choose whether to save those values to Before or After.
- Keeps the individual Assist buttons for single-field measurements.
- Keeps the Windows bundle artifact from v0.8.4.
- Updates app/build/installer/report versions to 0.9.0.

Upload steps:

1. Open your GitHub repository:
   https://github.com/spooled/cassette-motion-pro

2. Upload everything from this package to the repo root.

   Important:
   - Include the hidden .github folder.
   - The workflow file must end up here:

     .github/workflows/build.yml

3. Commit with this message:

   Add v0.9.0 guided landmark capture

4. Go to Actions.

5. Open the newest successful run.

6. Download:

   CassetteMotionPro-Windows-0.9.0

What to test:

1. Open Bike Fit Workspace.
2. Go to Bike Metrics.
3. Choose a Measurement image.
4. Click Guided Capture.
5. Click Calibrate Scale.
6. Click two points with a known real distance and enter that distance in mm.
7. Click Start Guided Capture.
8. Click the four landmarks in order:
   - Bottom bracket center
   - Saddle top
   - Saddle tip
   - Grip / hood contact point
9. Confirm the calculated values appear.
10. Click Save to Before or Save to After.
11. Confirm these fields are filled:
   - Saddle height
   - Saddle setback
   - Saddle tip to grip reach
   - Handlebar X
   - Handlebar Y
