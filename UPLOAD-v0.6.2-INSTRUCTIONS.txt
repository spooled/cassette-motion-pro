Cassette Motion Pro v0.6.2
==========================

This update adds a measurement reference image to Bike Metrics.

What changed:
- Adds a Measurement image control at the top of Bike Metrics.
- Lets you browse for a measurement image.
- Lets you reuse Before, After, or Side-by-side images as the measurement image.
- Saves the measurement image with the fit session.
- Shows the measurement image in generated reports.
- Updates Assist so it points toward the selected measurement image.

Upload steps:

1. Extract Cassette-Motion-Pro-0.6.2-measurement-reference-image.zip.
2. Open the extracted folder.
3. Upload everything INSIDE the extracted folder to the GitHub repository root.
4. Commit with: Add v0.6.2 measurement reference image

Then edit .github/workflows/build.yml and change VERSION to 0.6.2. Commit the
change and wait for the Actions run to receive a green check.

Test checklist:

1. Open a client.
2. Open the Bike Fit Workspace.
3. Open Bike Metrics.
4. Browse for a measurement image or click Use Before, Use After, or Use Side-by-side.
5. Click Assist and confirm it references the measurement image.
6. Generate a report and confirm Measurement Reference Image appears.
