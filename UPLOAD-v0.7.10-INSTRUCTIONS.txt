Cassette Motion Pro v0.7.10 - Before/After Video Side-by-side Fix

What changed:
- Fixes the Open Before + After video button.
- Before video opens on the left.
- After video opens on the right.
- The app creates two playback panes when needed.
- If a camera/capture pane is open, the app clears it so the two videos can open side by side.
- Keeps the v0.7.9 centered before/after reference image update.

How to upload:
1. Extract this zip.
2. Open your GitHub repo: https://github.com/spooled/cassette-motion-pro
3. Upload/replace these folders from the extracted package:
   - .github
   - docs
   - installer
   - src
4. Upload/replace the root files too if GitHub asks.
5. Commit with this message:
   Add v0.7.10 before after video side by side fix
6. Go to Actions and wait for the build to finish.
7. Download the Windows installer/portable artifact from the successful run.

What to test:
1. Open Bike Fit Workspace.
2. Choose a Before video and an After video.
3. Click Open Before + After.
4. Confirm Before is on the left and After is on the right.
5. Confirm the two videos are side by side.
