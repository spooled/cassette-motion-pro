Cassette Motion Pro v0.6.1
==========================

This update reshapes bike measurement into Bike Metrics.

What changed:
- Renames the measurement area to Bike Metrics.
- Removes the separate video-click measurement guide tab.
- Adds how-to-measure guidance directly beside each metric.
- Keeps Before and After input boxes in the same row.
- Adds Assist placeholders for future image-based guided measurement.

Upload steps:

1. Extract Cassette-Motion-Pro-0.6.1-bike-metrics.zip.
2. Open the extracted folder.
3. Upload everything INSIDE the extracted folder to the GitHub repository root.
4. Commit with: Add v0.6.1 bike metrics

Then edit .github/workflows/build.yml and change VERSION to 0.6.1. Commit the
change and wait for the Actions run to receive a green check.

Test checklist:

1. Open a client.
2. Open the Bike Fit Workspace.
3. Confirm there is a Bike Metrics tab.
4. Enter Before and After values.
5. Click Assist and confirm the future image-based workflow message appears.
6. Save, reopen the session, and confirm values remain saved.
