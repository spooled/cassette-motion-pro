Cassette Motion Pro v0.6.0
==========================

This update adds guided bike measurement workflows.

What changed:
- Adds a Bike Measurement Guide tab to the Bike Fit Workspace.
- Adds instructions for saddle height, saddle setback, saddle-tip-to-grip reach, Handlebar X/Y, handlebar reach, and handlebar drop.
- Adds Open Before and Open After buttons for each guided measurement.
- Opens the selected video and tells you which measurement field to fill in.
- Keeps the Measurements tab as the place where values are entered and saved.

Upload steps:

1. Extract Cassette-Motion-Pro-0.6.0-guided-bike-measurements.zip.
2. Open the extracted folder.
3. Upload everything INSIDE the extracted folder to the GitHub repository root.
4. Commit with: Add v0.6.0 guided bike measurements

Then edit .github/workflows/build.yml and change VERSION to 0.6.0. Commit the
change and wait for the Actions run to receive a green check.

Test checklist:

1. Open a client.
2. Open the Bike Fit Workspace.
3. Open the Bike Measurement Guide tab.
4. Click Open Before for Saddle height.
5. Confirm the instruction message appears.
6. Confirm the Before video opens.
7. Enter the measurement in the Measurements tab and save.
