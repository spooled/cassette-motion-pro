Cassette Motion Pro v0.10.2 - Wheelbase Bike Metric

This update makes wheelbase a permanent Bike Metrics value instead of just a
Guided Capture preview.

What changed:

- Keeps v0.10.1 editable Guided Capture landmarks.
- Adds Wheelbase as a Bike Metrics row with Before and After manual entry.
- Advanced Guided Capture now saves Wheelbase into Bike Metrics.
- Wheelbase appears in generated reports.
- Wheelbase image-assist uses horizontal measurement by default.
- Keeps Guided Capture report trace.
- Updates app/build/installer/report versions to 0.10.2.

Upload steps:

1. Open your GitHub repository:
   https://github.com/spooled/cassette-motion-pro

2. Upload everything from this package to the repo root.

   Important:
   - Include the hidden .github folder.
   - The workflow file must end up here:

     .github/workflows/build.yml

3. Commit with this message:

   Add v0.10.2 wheelbase bike metric

4. Go to Actions.

5. Open the newest run.

6. Download:

   CassetteMotionPro-Windows-0.10.2

What to test:

1. Open Bike Fit Workspace.
2. Go to Bike Metrics.
3. Choose a Measurement image.
4. Open Guided Capture.
5. Confirm Bike Metrics has a Wheelbase row.
6. Manually type a Wheelbase Before or After value, save, close, and reopen.
7. Confirm the Wheelbase value reloads.
8. Open Guided Capture.
9. Check Advanced landmarks.
10. Complete all 8 landmarks.
11. Confirm Wheelbase appears in the calculated values.
12. Save to Before or After.
13. Confirm the Wheelbase row fills in Bike Metrics.
14. Generate a report and confirm Wheelbase appears.
