Cassette Motion Pro v0.7.1
==========================

This update adds one-button Before + After image combine.

What changed:
- Adds a Combine Before + After button on the Report Images tab.
- Adds a Combine B+A button beside the Bike Metrics measurement image tools.
- Creates a side-by-side JPG from the selected Before image and After image.
- Saves the combined image inside the client's Photos folder.
- Automatically sets the combined image as the Side-by-side report image.
- Automatically sets the combined image as the Measurement Reference Image for Bike Metrics.

Upload steps:

1. Extract Cassette-Motion-Pro-0.7.1-combine-before-after-image.zip.
2. Open the extracted folder.
3. Upload everything INSIDE the extracted folder to the GitHub repository root.
4. Commit with: Add v0.7.1 before after image combine

Then edit .github/workflows/build.yml and change VERSION to 0.7.1. Commit the
change and wait for the Actions run to receive a green check.

Test checklist:

1. Open a client.
2. Open the Bike Fit Workspace.
3. Open Report Images.
4. Choose a Before image.
5. Choose an After image.
6. Click Combine Before + After.
7. Confirm the Side-by-side image field fills in.
8. Open Bike Metrics.
9. Confirm the Measurement image field also uses the combined side-by-side image.
10. Click Assist and confirm the combined image opens in the assistant.
