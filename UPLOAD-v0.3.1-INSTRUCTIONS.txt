Cassette Motion Pro v0.3.1 Update
==================================

This update simplifies the workspace to Left Video and Right Video.

1. Extract Cassette-Motion-Pro-0.3.1-update.zip.
2. Open the extracted folder. Upload its contents, not the outer folder.
3. In the GitHub repository choose Add file, then Upload files.
4. Drag everything from INSIDE the extracted folder onto GitHub.
5. Confirm paths begin with README.md, docs/, installer/, and src/.
6. Commit with: Simplify workspace to left and right videos

Then open:
https://github.com/spooled/cassette-motion-pro/blob/main/.github/workflows/build.yml

Click the pencil and change VERSION: 0.3.0 to VERSION: 0.3.1, then commit.
Download the 0.3.1 installer after the final Actions run receives a green check.
