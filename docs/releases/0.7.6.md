# Cassette Motion Pro 0.7.6

This release adds zoom and pan support to the Image Measurement Assistant.

## What changed

- Keeps the v0.7.5 horizontal Saddle Setback tool.
- Adds **Zoom In**, **Zoom Out**, and **Reset Zoom** controls.
- Adds mouse-wheel zoom over the measurement image.
- Adds right-click drag or middle-click drag panning.
- Keeps calibration and measurement point coordinates accurate while zoomed.

## Why it matters

Bike fit landmarks can be small: bottom bracket center, saddle nose, grip
center, and handlebar reference points all need precise clicks. Zoom and pan
make those clicks much easier to place accurately.
