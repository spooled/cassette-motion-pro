# Cassette Motion Pro v0.9.8

## Scrollable Guided Capture

This release fixes the Guided Capture window layout on smaller screens.

### Changes

- Makes the Guided Capture right-side controls vertically scrollable.
- Keeps **Save to Before** and **Save to After** reachable even when the
  instruction/results panel is taller than the window.
- Keeps the **Close** button pinned at the bottom of the right side.
- Keeps v0.9.7 Guided Capture report trace behavior.
- Updates build, installer, app, and report versions to `0.9.8`.

### Test checklist

1. Open Bike Fit Workspace.
2. Go to Bike Metrics.
3. Choose a Measurement image.
4. Open Guided Capture.
5. Resize the window shorter if needed.
6. Confirm the right side scrolls.
7. Confirm **Save to Before** and **Save to After** are reachable.
8. Complete a Guided Capture and save it.
