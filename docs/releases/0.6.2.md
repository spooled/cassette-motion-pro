# Cassette Motion Pro 0.6.2

This release adds a measurement reference image to Bike Metrics.

## Changes

- Adds a **Measurement image** control at the top of Bike Metrics.
- Lets the fitter browse for a measurement image.
- Lets the fitter reuse the Before, After, or Side-by-side report image as the measurement image.
- Saves the measurement image with the fit session.
- Shows the measurement image in generated reports.
- Updates Assist messaging to reference the selected measurement image.

## Test checklist

1. Open a client.
2. Open the Bike Fit Workspace.
3. Open **Bike Metrics**.
4. Browse for a measurement image, or click **Use Before**, **Use After**, or **Use Side-by-side**.
5. Click **Assist** and confirm it references the measurement image.
6. Generate a report and confirm the Measurement Reference Image section appears.
7. Reopen the session and confirm the measurement image is still saved.
