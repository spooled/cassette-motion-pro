# Cassette Motion Pro v0.10.1

## Editable Guided Capture landmarks

This release improves measurement accuracy by letting users adjust placed
Guided Capture landmarks before saving.

### Changes

- Keeps v0.10.0 Basic and Advanced landmark modes.
- Allows left-dragging placed orange bike landmarks to fine-tune their position.
- Recalculates values after dragging a completed landmark set.
- Shows a hand cursor when hovering over an editable landmark.
- Shows a bright selection ring around the point being dragged.
- Keeps right-drag/middle-drag panning behavior.
- Updates build, installer, app, and report versions to `0.10.1`.

### Test checklist

1. Open Bike Fit Workspace.
2. Go to Bike Metrics.
3. Choose a Measurement image.
4. Open Guided Capture.
5. Calibrate Scale and complete Basic Guided Capture.
6. Drag one of the orange landmarks.
7. Confirm the values update after dragging.
8. Save to Before or After.
9. Repeat in Advanced landmarks mode.
