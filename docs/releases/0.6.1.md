# Cassette Motion Pro 0.6.1

This release reshapes the measurement workflow into Bike Metrics.

## Changes

- Renames the measurement area to **Bike Metrics**.
- Removes the separate video-click measurement guide tab.
- Adds measurement guidance directly beside each metric.
- Keeps Before and After input boxes in the same row.
- Adds **Assist** placeholders for the future image-based measurement assistant.

## Test checklist

1. Open a client.
2. Open the Bike Fit Workspace.
3. Confirm there is a **Bike Metrics** tab.
4. Confirm Saddle height, setback, reach, Handlebar X/Y, and other rows have guidance.
5. Enter Before and After values.
6. Click **Assist** and confirm it explains the future image-based workflow.
7. Save, reopen the session, and confirm values remain saved.
