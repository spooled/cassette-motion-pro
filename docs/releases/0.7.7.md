# Cassette Motion Pro 0.7.7

This release fixes signed measurement entry for values such as `-9 mm`.

## What changed

- Keeps the v0.7.6 zoom/pan measurement assistant.
- Adds **Enter Manual Value** to the Image Measurement Assistant.
- Manual values may be positive or negative.
- Values such as `-9` save as `-9.0 mm`.
- Clarifies that calibration length must remain positive because it is used only
  for scale.

## Why it matters

Saddle setback often needs a signed value. Calibration should always use a
positive known length, but the final fit measurement may be negative.
