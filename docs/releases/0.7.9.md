# Cassette Motion Pro v0.7.9

## Centered before/after measurement reference image

This update improves the automatic **Combine Before + After** image used for reports and Bike Metrics measurements.

### Changes

- Before and After photos are now placed into equal-size side-by-side panels.
- Each photo automatically scales to fit inside its panel.
- Each photo is centered inside its panel.
- The combined side-by-side image remains usable as the Bike Metrics measurement reference image.
- Keeps v0.7.8 zoom centering inside the Image Measurement Assistant.

### Test checklist

1. Open the Bike Fit Workspace.
2. Go to Report Images.
3. Choose a Before image and an After image.
4. Click **Combine Before + After**.
5. Confirm the generated side-by-side image shows both photos centered in equal panels.
6. Go to Bike Metrics and confirm the Measurement image is set to the combined side-by-side image.
7. Open a metric assist tool and confirm zoom/pan/Center Image still works.
