# Cassette Motion Pro v0.7.10

## Before/After video side-by-side fix

This update fixes the **Open Before + After** button in the Bike Fit Workspace.

### Changes

- Opens the Before video in the left playback screen.
- Opens the After video in the right playback screen.
- Creates two playback screens when needed.
- Clears non-video panes before opening the side-by-side comparison.
- Keeps v0.7.9 centered before/after reference image behavior.

### Test checklist

1. Open the Bike Fit Workspace.
2. Choose a Before video.
3. Choose an After video.
4. Click **Open Before + After**.
5. Confirm the app shows two video panes side by side.
6. Confirm Before is on the left and After is on the right.
