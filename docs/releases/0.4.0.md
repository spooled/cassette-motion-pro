# Cassette Motion Pro 0.4.0

Version 0.4.0 adds guided body-angle measurement to the Bike Fit Workspace.

- New Body Angles tab with Before and After values
- Knee, hip, ankle, torso, shoulder, and elbow measurements
- Measure Before Video and Measure After Video launch buttons
- Automatically activates the existing Bike Fit posture overlay on the video
- Extended posture overlay shows all six angles together
- Angle values, videos, bike measurements, goals, and notes remain attached to
  the persistent client session
