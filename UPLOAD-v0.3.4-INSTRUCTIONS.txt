Cassette Motion Pro v0.3.4 Update
==================================

This update adds Handlebar X and Handlebar Y to the Measurements chart.

1. Extract Cassette-Motion-Pro-0.3.4-update.zip.
2. Open the extracted folder and upload its contents, not the outer folder.
3. In GitHub choose Add file, then Upload files.
4. Drag everything from INSIDE the extracted folder onto GitHub.
5. Confirm paths begin with README.md, docs/, installer/, and src/.
6. Commit with: Add Handlebar X and Y measurements

Then edit .github/workflows/build.yml and change VERSION: 0.3.3 to
VERSION: 0.3.4. Commit the change and download the 0.3.4 installer after the
final Actions run receives a green check.
