Cassette Motion Pro v0.9.9 - Guided Capture Clarity

This update makes Guided Capture easier to follow while you click bike
measurement points.

What changed:

- Keeps the v0.9.8 scrollable Guided Capture side panel.
- Adds a clearer “next point” hint under the current point.
- Adds an on-image prompt showing what point to click next.
- Adds a highlighted crosshair around the mouse while capture mode is active.
- Makes clicked landmark dots and labels larger/easier to read.
- Keeps Guided Capture report trace.
- Keeps the saddle setback convention:
  - Behind BB = negative
  - In front of BB = positive
- Updates app/build/installer/report versions to 0.9.9.

Upload steps:

1. Open your GitHub repository:
   https://github.com/spooled/cassette-motion-pro

2. Upload everything from this package to the repo root.

   Important:
   - Include the hidden .github folder.
   - The workflow file must end up here:

     .github/workflows/build.yml

3. Commit with this message:

   Add v0.9.9 guided capture clarity

4. Go to Actions.

5. Open the newest run.

6. Download:

   CassetteMotionPro-Windows-0.9.9

What to test:

1. Open Bike Fit Workspace.
2. Go to Bike Metrics.
3. Choose a Measurement image.
4. Open Guided Capture.
5. Calibrate Scale.
6. Optional: set Level Reference.
7. Start Guided Capture.
8. Confirm the side panel clearly says which point is next.
9. Move the mouse over the image and confirm the crosshair cue appears.
10. Click all four landmarks and confirm the saved point labels are easier to read.
11. Scroll the right panel and confirm Save to Before / Save to After are reachable.
12. Save a Guided Capture and generate a report.
13. Confirm the Measurement Capture Trace table still appears.
